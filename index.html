Calcutta Auction Explained

After the blind draw teams are decided, we will do an auction. You can choose to bid on any team auction style. 80% of the winning bids will go to a prize pool and 20% will be used for event expenses. Whoever bids on the winning team will get 75% of that prize pool, 25% of the prize pool will go to the person who chose a randomly selected team. We will do this for both of the blind draws and Trios so bring some cash but be smart about your bids! We will accept Venmo and similar payment options for these bids.

50/50 Raffle Auction

There will be a weekend long 50/50 Raffle – You will get a physical ticket and you will not need to be present for the draw. 50% of this pot will go direct to the winner and 50% will go to event expenses.

Ticket Pricing: $5 per ticket · $20 for 6 tickets · $100 for 40 tickets.
